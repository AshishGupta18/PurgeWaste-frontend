PurgeWaste is a waste management tool that is our first step towards a cleaner India. We want to shorten the waste recycling process's supply chain.

What we do

Making recycling more accessible for people and profitable for waste collectors is our aim.

How we do

We do this by digitising the procedure, and paying the waste collectors a formal wage.

What are we improving

We are shortening the chain and bringing convenience to this unspoken niche industry.

Perks of users

Making the process simpler by digitising it, enabling home pickup, and eliminating price haggling.

How we are hleping society

Waste Management is the area in India that needs the most assistance but is the least talked about, we aim to change that.
